### 1. Why LangChain?

* LLMs have **limitations**:

  * Limited context
  * Outdated knowledge base
  * Data privacy issues
  * High cost
  * No direct connection with 3rd party tools
* Data sources: `.txt`, `.pdf`, `.csv`, `.sql`, `.xls`, `.html`, `.png`, etc.
* LangChain allows integration of LLMs with **data, tools, and APIs**.
